About SchemePro

Your AI assistant for Kenya curriculum planning

SchemePro is a web-based application designed to help Kenyan teachers generate professional, KICD-compliant schemes of work in minutes. Whether you teach in a primary school, junior school, or secondary school, our AI generates complete term plans with topics, objectives, learning activities, teaching aids, references and assessment methods — all aligned to the Ministry of Education standards.


The platform supports both the 8-4-4 system and the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC/CBE), producing the correct table format for each. Documents can be downloaded as PDF, Word (.docx), or ZIP bundles.
