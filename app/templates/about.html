About SchemePro

AI-powered scheme of work generation built specifically for Kenyan teachers and schools

What is SchemePro?

SchemePro is a web application that helps Kenyan teachers generate professional, KICD-compliant schemes of work in minutes. Whether you teach in a primary school, junior school or secondary school, our AI (powered by Anthropic's Claude) generates complete term plans with topics, objectives, learning activities, teaching aids, references and assessment methods — all aligned to the Ministry of Education standards.


The platform supports both the 8-4-4 system (Primary and Secondary) and the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC/CBE) for Junior School (Grade 7–9), producing the correct table format for each. Documents download as PDF, Word (.docx), or a ZIP bundle.
